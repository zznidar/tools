pdb Missing Residues Contig Maker

This tool checks the PDB for missing residues, then outputs a contig you can use in Prosculpt. For example: if your .PDB is missing residues 6 and 7, this tool will output something like [A1-5/2/A8-99]. This way, you can avoid the AssertionError: ('A', 6) is not in pdb file!

Always double-check your desired hotspots are not missing and/or affected by inpainted residues!

Output will be shown here. Copy only relevant parts.


Note: If your .pdb contains insertion codes, use PDB Insertion Codes Remover first to renumber your residues. Do note that residue numbers might change after that -- always observe your structure.
